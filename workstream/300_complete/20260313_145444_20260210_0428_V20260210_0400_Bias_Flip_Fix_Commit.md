## 2. Plan of Approach

2.  **Switching Enforcement**: Modify the API to explicitly REMOVE `ai_target` strategies that do not match the new bias, rather than just appending new ones.
3.  **State Archiving**: Call `_archive_grid_live()` before any deactivation or grid modification to preserve a history trail (`grid_live_history`).
4.  **UI Sync**: Update `ai_picker.html` to trigger deactivation immediately upon detecting a bias flip.
5.  **Data Cleanup**: Prune `grid_live.json` and `activations.json` of stale or corrupted entries.
