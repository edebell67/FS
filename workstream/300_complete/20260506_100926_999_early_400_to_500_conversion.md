## Conclusions

### Key Findings

1. **Headline conversion is misleading**
   - 57.1% overall, but many entries are already at $500+
   - True $400-$449 entry conversion: **33.3%**

2. **Strategy type matters significantly**
   - Base: 77.8% conversion
   - R: 66.7% conversion
   - **R_Rev: 11.1% conversion** (avoid)

3. **Pair selection critical**
   - AUD: 100% conversion
   - GBPAUD_C: 0% conversion

4. **Small sample size warning**
   - Only 35 strategies qualified across 7 days
   - Results may not be statistically robust

5. **Date inconsistency**
   - Ranges from 25% (2026-05-04) to 100% (2026-04-28)
   - Not a reliable standalone signal

### Actionable Insight

**If using $400 by 5am as entry signal:**
- Prefer: Base or R strategies
- Prefer: AUD, CHF, NZDAUD_C pairs
- **Avoid: R_Rev strategies, GBPAUD_C**
- Entry at exactly $400-$449 has only 33% success rate

### Limitations

- Small sample size (n=35)
- Only 5 days with qualifying strategies
- Extreme momentum values skew averages
