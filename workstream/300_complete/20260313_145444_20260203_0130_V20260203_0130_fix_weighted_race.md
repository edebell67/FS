# Fix Weighted Race History Instability

## 1. Understanding of Requirements
The user reported that `weighted_race.py` generates unstable historical snapshots in `_frequency.json`. The "leaders" and "net" values for past time buckets change on subsequent runs. This happens because the script currently processes **Open Trades** (which have fluctuating P&L) and attributes them to their `entry_time`. As the open trade's P&L updates, the historical bucket corresponding to its entry time is overwritten with the new value, violating the expectation of a static history.

## 2. Plan of Approach
To ensure historical stability (snapshots not changing once captured):
1.  **Filter for Closed Trades Only**: Modify `load_trades` to strictly ignore any trade that does not have an `exit_time`.
2.  **Use Exit Time**: Use the `exit_time` as the timestamp for the race. This ensures that P&L is only counted when it is realized. Since closed trades are immutable, the history will remain static.
