# Plan: Update Auto-Activation Criteria - Trade Count and Win Rate

This document outlines the plan to enhance the auto-activation logic in the backend by requiring a minimum number of trades and a minimum win rate (percentage of profitable trades).

## 1. Understanding of Requirements

*   **Criteria 1**: The strategy must have at least `min_auto_trades` (default: 3).
*   **Criteria 2**: The strategy must have a win rate (profitable trades / total trades) > `min_auto_win_rate` (default: 85%).
*   **Today's Scope**: These metrics are calculated based on all trades from the current calendar day (including virtual trades).

## 2. Plan of Approach

1.  **Modify `config.json`**:
    *   Add `"min_auto_trades": 3`.
    *   Add `"min_auto_win_rate": 85`.
2.  **Modify `common.py`**:
    *   In `_perform_auto_activation_check`:
        *   Retrieve the new configuration parameters.
        *   Update `global_stats` aggregation to also count profitable trades (where `net_return > 0`).
        *   Calculate the strategy-level `total_count` and `total_profitable_count` (summing across all products).
        *   Calculate `win_rate = (total_profitable_count / total_count) * 100`.
        *   Update the candidacy filters for both **Net** and **Alt** paths to include:
            *   `total_count >= min_auto_trades`
            *   `win_rate > min_auto_win_rate`
            *   *(Continuing to require `total_pnl > threshold_y`)*
3.  **Update Version**:
    *   Update `VERSION` in `constants.py` to `V20251231_1445`.
