# Epic: Trading Strategy Warehouse Marketing Engine

## Source
- User requirement on 2026-03-16:

  - create a marketing process to develop an online following using social media
  - create a medium (landing page) through which users can subscribe
  - the system should be 95% autonomous
  - key objectives:
    - increase the product profile/reach online aggressively
    - increase the subscriber base week to week

## Epic Summary
- Build a growth system around `trading_strategy_warehouse` that combines positioning, content production, social distribution, subscription capture, analytics, and optimization with high autonomy and measurable weekly growth.

## Product
- `trading_strategy_warehouse`

## Primary Objectives
- Increase online reach and visibility aggressively.
- Increase week-to-week subscriber growth.

## Operating Constraint
- Target operating model: `95% autonomous`
- This implies strong automation for planning, asset generation, scheduling, analytics, and optimization, while retaining human control for approval-sensitive, brand-sensitive, or platform-risk actions where needed.

## Success Metrics
- Reach growth:
  - impressions
  - follower growth
  - landing page visits
  - content publishing consistency
- Subscriber growth:
  - weekly new subscribers
  - landing page conversion rate
  - subscriber retention / churn
- Automation:
  - percentage of campaign workflow executed without manual intervention
  - failure rate of content/scheduling/publishing pipeline

## Solution Areas
- Offer and positioning
- Landing page and subscription funnel
- Social media content engine
- Publishing and scheduling automation
- Subscriber capture and lifecycle management
- Analytics and growth dashboard
- Optimization loop
- Governance, approval, and platform safety guardrails
