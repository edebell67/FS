# EP038 Product One-Pager — Live Spread Intelligence

## Product

A live digital information product that continuously finds, scores, and presents marketplace pricing spreads.

The primary offer is **access to information**: live spread data, source/marketplace research, timestamps, confidence scores, and risk notes. Any internal trading/testing is secondary and only used to improve the reliability of the information product.

## Core promise

Give resellers and opportunity hunters a live board of active pricing gaps, showing where a product/asset appears to sell for more on one marketplace than it can be acquired for elsewhere.

## Product form

```text
Live Spread Board
```

Each spread is presented as a live product card:

- item/product;
- category;
- marketplace price;
- lower-cost source price;
- gross spread;
- estimated net margin range;
- state: discovered / watching / live / cooling / expired / avoid;
- last checked timestamp;
- confidence score;
- risk notes;
- source and marketplace links.

## What customers pay for

They pay for:

- time saved;
- cross-market discovery;
- recurring spread monitoring;
- source memory;
- price-spike detection;
- risk filtering;
- alerts when spreads become active.

## What we do not sell

We do not sell guaranteed profit or instructions to transact.

Correct positioning:

```text
candidate spread intelligence
live marketplace research feed
pricing-gap watchlist
verification required before action
```

## MVP

Create a manually maintained board with 10 candidate spreads and update them over repeated checks. The goal is to prove whether continuously refreshed spread intelligence feels valuable before automating.

## Success signal

A customer says:

```text
I would pay to monitor this board because it saves me time and shows opportunities I would not have found quickly myself.
```
