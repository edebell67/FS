# EP038 — Primary Business Objective: Information Product

Created: 2026-06-27

## Primary objective

EP038's primary business is the **provision of pricing-spread information** as a digital product.

The core product is not direct trading, dropshipping, or arbitrage execution.

The core product is:

```text
Live pricing-spread intelligence
```

We provide information about observed marketplace spreads:

- where products/assets appear to sell for more;
- where they may be sourced for less;
- how large the observed spread is;
- how fresh the observation is;
- how risky/credible the spread appears;
- whether the spread is recurring, live, cooling, expired, or avoid.

## What customers buy

Customers buy access to:

```text
live spread information
+ source/marketplace research
+ confidence scoring
+ risk notes
+ historical spread movement
+ alerts/watchlists later
```

They are not buying guaranteed profit.

They are not buying a managed trading service.

They are not being told to transact.

## Trading/testing role

EP038 may do limited internal trading/testing later, but only for validation.

Purpose of test trading:

- check whether identified spreads are real;
- validate fees, delivery, fulfilment, returns, and stock risk;
- improve confidence scoring;
- improve risk notes;
- prove whether marketplace signals translate into executable opportunities.

Test trading is **secondary** and must not become the public promise.

## Public positioning

Use:

```text
pricing-spread information product
marketplace intelligence feed
live spread board
candidate opportunity research
reseller intelligence
spread monitoring
```

Avoid:

```text
we trade for you
we guarantee profit
risk-free arbitrage
copy our trades
buy this and earn
```

## Website framing

The website should present itself as:

```text
A live information product for people who want to monitor marketplace pricing spreads.
```

Each spread card should make clear:

```text
Information only — verify before acting.
```

## Compliance wording

Default disclaimer:

```text
EP038 provides pricing-spread research and market information only. It does not guarantee profit and does not instruct users to buy, sell, list, or transact. Prices, stock, fees, delivery, resale rights, authenticity, and platform rules must be independently verified before any action.
```

## Operating rule

When in doubt, frame EP038 as:

```text
information provision
```

not:

```text
trading execution
```

## Success definition

EP038 succeeds if people pay for access to reliable, continuously updated spread intelligence.

Internal test trades may improve the product, but the product remains the information layer.
