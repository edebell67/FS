# EP038 — Digital-First Marketplace Arbitrage

Objective: build a research database of marketplace products with resale demand, prioritising digital products, and identify lower-cost acquisition sources only where resale/platform rights make the opportunity viable.

## Current proof-of-process files

- `EP038_OBJECTIVE.md` — project objective and guardrails.
- `data/product_candidates.csv` — candidate product tracker.
- `data/acquisition_sources.csv` — lower-cost source tracker.
- `data/price_history.csv` — observed listing/sold-price history.
- `spikes/001-digital-policy-gates/` — policy/compliance research.
- `spikes/002-candidate-category-shortlist/` — category selection.

## Guardrail

No live purchases, listings, automated scraping at scale, or marketplace contact without explicit approval.
