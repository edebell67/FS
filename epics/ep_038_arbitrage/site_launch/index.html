Product mechanics

What subscribers buy

Live spread board

Spread cards show marketplace price, source price, gross spread, state, confidence, risk and expiry pressure.

Preference alerts

Subscribers choose categories, thresholds, risk ceiling and expiry windows. Matching spreads become would-send alert events.

Time-sensitive value

The board highlights how much identified spread value may disappear soon due to auctions, closeouts or stock movement.

Current launch state

Static-host ready, data-driven pages.

The launch package is designed for GitHub Pages or any static host. It renders from JSON data and has client-side filtering and preference capture for early-access validation.
