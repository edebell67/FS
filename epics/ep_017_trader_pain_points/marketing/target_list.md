# EP017 Marketing Target Communities

This list identifies the communities on Reddit and hashtags on X (Twitter) for the landing page series outreach.

## Reddit Subreddits
Targeting traders who discuss strategy, edge, and information overload.

| Subreddit | Why? | Tone / Approach |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **r/Daytrading** | Large, active community of retail traders. | Value-focused discussion about performance. |
| **r/Forex** | Specific to currency traders. | Deep dives into technical edge and momentum. |
| **r/algorithmictrading** | Focused on data, automation, and backtesting. | Highly technical, transparency-focused. |
| **r/Trading** | General trading community. | General interest in curation and efficiency. |
| **r/pennystocks** | High-momentum traders. | Quick, high-impact momentum detection. |

## X (Twitter) Hashtags
Targeting #FinTwit with high-precision signals and data.

- **Primary:** #FinTwit, #TradingSignals, #AlgoTrading
- **Secondary:** #Forex, #DayTrading, #PriceAction, #CryptoTrading
- **Topics:** #DataAnalytics, #TradingStrategy, #MarketRegime

## Outreach Guidelines
1.  **Participate First:** Comment on relevant threads before posting links.
2.  **Transparency:** Explicitly mention that we are testing these "feeds" and looking for feedback on the pain points.
3.  **Value-First:** Every post must describe the problem (e.g., "I got tired of guru noise") before offering the link as a solution.
