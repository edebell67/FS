02What we collect

When you fill in the demo form, we collect:

  • your business name and your name;
  • the phone number you give us to call;

If we call you, we may also keep a record of the call and any notes from it (for example, that you asked us not to call again).

Call you (including by automated or AI voice call) about your demo and setting up the serviceYour consent — the box you ticked. Automated and AI voice calls require your prior consent under PECR, and you can withdraw it at any time.

04The demo call, and your right to stop it

The demo call may be made using an automated or AI voice. You agreed to this on the form, and you can change your mind at any moment — there’s no penalty, and it won’t affect anything else.

To stop us calling, just tell the caller, reply to any message from us, or email [EMAIL]. We’ll remove your number from our calling list straight away and add it to our own do-not-call record so it isn’t used again.

Before making live marketing calls we also screen numbers against the Telephone Preference Service (TPS) and Corporate Telephone Preference Service (CTPS). Asking us to stop always takes priority over anything else.

08Your rights

Under UK data-protection law you can ask us to:

  • show you the information we hold about you;
  • correct it if it’s wrong;
  • delete it;
  • stop or limit how we use it;
  • hand it over in a portable format;
  • stop using it where we rely on legitimate interests; and
  • withdraw any consent you’ve given — at any time, without affecting what we did before you withdrew it.

To use any of these, email [EMAIL]. It’s free, and we’ll reply within one month.
